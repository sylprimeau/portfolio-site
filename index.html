Superconvert

Superconvert is a web app I built to "solve my own problems". I love cooking and every time I wanted to follow a recipe, I'd have to convert everything from Imperial to Metric. Existing sites required 7 or 8 actions for each conversion. What a pain! So I made Superconvert, which converts up to 30 unit pairs at a time. Users can also customize their home screen colors and unit pairs.
